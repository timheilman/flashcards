The set of all entry words is equal to the union of all
15-letter-or-shorter words in enable1, wwf4, otcwl2, all two letter
additions to otcwl2 in otcwl2014, and all three-from-two additions to
otcwl2 in otcwl2014, per
http://www.scrabbleplayers.org/w/Official_Tournament_and_Club_Word_List

Every inflection in a definition is in union(otcwl2, otcwl2014-2LW,
otcwl2014-3F2), and all inflections in definitions in the SOWPODS
dictionary which are in that union appear in the corresponding
definitions here.

[+|-]e1: does or doesn't appear in enable1
[+|-]w4: does or doesn't appear in words with friends (tm) version 4
[+|-]o2: does or doesn't appear in otcwl2
[+|-]o3: does or doesn't appear in otcwl2014 (sometimes missing; no electronic copy yet)
[+|-]r??: SOWPODS root for this SOWPODS inflection word does or doesn't appear in corresponding word list
